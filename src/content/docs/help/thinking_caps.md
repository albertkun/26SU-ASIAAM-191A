## Part 1: Posting your own discussion

:::caution
Due at 3:15pm PST on Mondays, but to be eligible for extra credit you must submit at **3:15pm PST on Sundays**.
:::
1. Make sure you are signed into your GitHub account. <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap0.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap0.png">
2. Go to the [course website](https://albertkun.github.io/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/)
3. Click on "**Discussions**" <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap1.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap1.png">
4. Click on the "**Thinking Cap**" you are posting for <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap3.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap3.png">
5. Scroll down to find the comment box: <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap4.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap4.png">
6. Respond to the prompt for the week. <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap_response.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap5.png">
7. Click **Comment** to submit: <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap5.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap5.png">
8. The first part of assignment is now complete!

## Part 2: Responding to discussions

9. Go to the discussion page on the GitHub repository

10. Try to look for student posts with no replies. <img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap8.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap8.png">

11. Type a thoughtful reply and click **Comment** .<img src="/26SU-ASIAAM-191A/help/media/thinkingcap9.png" alt="./media/thinkingcap9.png">

12. Repeat **Steps #8 - #10** for another student.

13. Congratulations on finishing the assignment!
